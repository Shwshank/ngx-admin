Sawtooth Project

Introduction

Goverment of India has a centralized bidding platform for all vendors accross the country. The platform is GEM i.e Govt. E-Marketplace. NABL i.e National Accreditation Board for Laboratories is a govt. body that provides accreditation to laboratories accross the nation. In order to submit a bid by vendor on GEM, it is mandat that the product that vendor is bidding should be tested by one of the lab certified by NABL.

The issue

The process of submitting a bid is simple for a vendor. Vendor just needs to fill a form mentioning deatils of product along with the report of lab certificate.

Here comes the twist, the verification process of certificates used to be simple as well untill recent months when the number of applicatins exceeds 5000 per months. GEM has to maually verify each and every certificate. And validation of one certificate my take some 'n' no. of weeks!

How sawtooth is helping the process

Labs that have been accredited by NABL has a unique identifier id for each lab. And every certificate issued by the lab has an id with initials as the id that is being issued by NABL. Labs are provided with a CRM system that is integrated with a sawtooth application layer.

All of the ids and certificates involved in the process are recorded and are part of the chain consists of all the records and participants.

While applying for a bid in GEM, the vendor has to enter the certificate number that is issued by the lab after testing the product. The platform used by GEM has APIs integrated by which the request for verification of ids and certificates are initiated.
